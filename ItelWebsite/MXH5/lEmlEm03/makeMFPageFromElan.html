Suppose we have a unit MD1, with video MD1.mp4; we can simply say 
   avconv -i MD1.mp4 MD1.webm
and that might do it, or we start playing with params. If we have audio MD1.wav and no video, we need to pick a picture pic.png and say 
   avconv -loop 1 -i pic.png -i MD1.wav -shortest MD1.webm
